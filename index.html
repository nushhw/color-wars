Color Wars

A turn-based territory control game where you try to dominate the grid by triggering explosive chain reactions!

Blue Player's Turn

Game Rules

  • Objective: Be the last player with dots on the board.
  • Turns: Players alternate turns clicking on grid cells.
  • Placing Dots:
    • Click an empty cell to place 3 dots of your color
    • Click your own cell to add 1 dot
  • Explosions: When a cell reaches 4 dots, it explodes:
    • Sends 1 dot to each adjacent cell (up, down, left, right)
    • If an enemy cell is hit, it converts to your color
    • Explosions can trigger chain reactions
